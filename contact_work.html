Experience

Que SeRaw Seraw

Que SeRaw Seraw is a raw and vegan takeout restaurant located in Burlingame. It hosts a wide variety of food with a revolving menu of delicious and healthy meals. I’ve worked there for 4 years and I am currently working there now. Because this restaurant is a small local place I’m often left with a wide variety of tasks. On an average day, you can find me serving customers, preparing ingredients, cooking dishes, putting together orders, tracking inventory, and cleaning. There’s never a dull moment, and there’s always work to be done. Because of this, I've learned to work efficiently and stay focused on my goals.

Sequoia Foods

Sequoia Foods is a small grocery store in Burlingame. I worked here for about ten weeks and gained a lot of valuable experience. Similarly to Que Seraw, this was a smaller shop where I was doing a little bit of everything, such as: Ringing up customers, Tracking inventory, checking expiration dates, scooping ice cream, cleaning, and facing. Although definitely less hectic than Que Seraw, there’s still always work to be done, and because I worked alone, I had to set tasks for myself to complete while paying attention to our customers and products.

Graphic Design
